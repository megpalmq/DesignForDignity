Project 6: Exercise and Food Synergy

Exercise and Food Synergy: Connection between physical activity and nutrition; how regular exercise helps the elderly maintain muscle mass and energy levels when paired with proper diet. Create a section on simple exercises that can be done at home or outdoors, paired with suggestions on post-exercise recovery meals to support mobility and strength.

Infographic Poster

About the Project

This project focuses on the synergy between exercise and nutrition, emphasizing the importance of regular physical activity for elderly individuals. It highlights how exercise, when paired with a proper diet, can help maintain muscle mass, improve energy levels, and enhance overall mobility. The posters provide simple, accessible exercises that seniors can do at home or outdoors, catering to various fitness levels. Additionally, we include suggestions for post-exercise recovery meals, offering nutrient-dense options that support muscle recovery, joint health, and strength-building, ensuring seniors remain active and healthy as they age.

Design Process

  1. Research & Discovery: Our team started by researching the best practices for elderly fitness and the role of nutrition in post-exercise recovery. We focused on exercises that are easy on the joints, while also promoting muscle strength and flexibility. In parallel, we examined the nutritional needs of seniors, identifying foods rich in protein, healthy fats, and essential vitamins and minerals to support muscle recovery.
  2. Concept Development:With insights from our research, we conceptualized a series of informative and visually appealing posters. The key focus was on creating an educational yet engaging design. We selected simple exercises such as chair squats, arm raises, and gentle stretching movements, ensuring they could be easily performed by seniors with varying mobility levels. We also paired these exercises with recovery meal suggestions like smoothies, lean meats, and anti-inflammatory foods.
  3. Prototype Testing: The design was tested for readability and clarity, especially considering the needs of older adults. Large fonts, high-contrast colors, and easy-to-understand icons were incorporated to ensure the posters were both visually appealing and accessible. We also included step-by-step visuals for exercises, ensuring that the instructions were clear and easy to follow.
  4. Final Solution: The final posters were polished with a clean layout, bold colors, and engaging images of seniors performing exercises. Each poster featured a clear section for exercise instructions and another for post-exercise meal suggestions.
