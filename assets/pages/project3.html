Impact of Diet on Blood Pressure and Cardiovascular Health? How does blood Pressure relate to diet?

This project focuses on creating two infographic posters that highlight the impact of diet on blood pressure and cardiovascular health, with an emphasis on the dietary needs of seniors. The first poster outlines the importance of a heart-healthy diet, including low-sodium foods and the benefits of the DASH Diet (Dietary Approaches to Stop Hypertension) for managing hypertension. The second poster features foods to avoid, such as processed snacks high in sodium, and offers healthy alternatives, including nutrient-rich herbs and whole foods. The goal of these posters is to educate seniors on how diet can effectively manage blood pressure and improve overall heart health.
