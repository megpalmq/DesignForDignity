About the Project

This collaborative project was aimed at addressing food insecurity and sustainability. We worked with local communities in Seoul and Indianapolis to develop a design that would encourage sustainable food practices while ensuring that vulnerable populations had access to healthy meals.

Design Process

  1. Research & Discovery: We spent weeks gathering data from local food banks and community centers, interviewing residents and analyzing food insecurity in both cities.
  2. Concept Development: After gathering insights, we brainstormed several solutions and created early prototypes using user-centered design principles.
  3. Prototype Testing: We tested our solutions with real users, gathered feedback, and refined our designs to meet the needs of the communities.
  4. Final Solution: The final design focused on creating a food distribution platform with an easy-to-use interface and accessibility features for elderly residents.
