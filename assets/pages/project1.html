Food Access in Indianapolis and Songdo/Seoul

This collaborative project was aimed at addressing food insecurity and sustainability. We worked with local communities in Indianapolis to develop a design that would encourage sustainable food practices while ensuring that vulnerable populations had access to healthy meals.
