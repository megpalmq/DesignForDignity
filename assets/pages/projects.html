Deliverables

Yonsei University and Herron School of Art and Design students united to create impactful designs addressing food scarcity and health challenges for elderly communities in Korea and Indianapolis. Each team was assigned a distinct topic and presentation style, using visuals, typography, and layout to communicate the message effectively.
