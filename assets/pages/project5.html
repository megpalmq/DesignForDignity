Brochure

About the Project

This brochure focuses on the importance of proper hydration for elderly health, emphasizing the benefits of staying hydrated and providing practical tips for seniors to improve their water intake. It covers common hydration challenges in seniors and offers solutions such as incorporating hydrating foods and beverages into their daily routine, as well as understanding the signs of dehydration and how to prevent it.

Design Process

  1. Research & Discovery: We began by researching the risks and challenges seniors face in staying hydrated. This included understanding how age affects hydration levels and the most effective ways to encourage water intake for older adults.
  2. Concept Development:With insights from our research, we identified key tips and strategies to include in the brochure. We aimed to provide clear, actionable advice to help seniors make hydration a priority, such as incorporating hydrating foods and setting regular water-drinking schedules.
  3. Prototype Testing: The brochures were designed with large fonts, high-contrast colors, and simple visuals to ensure readability for older adults. Icons representing hydration and wellness were used to enhance clarity and visual appeal.
  4. Final Solution: The brochures were finalized with a clean layout and engaging graphics to encourage seniors to make hydration a regular part of their health routine. We also ensured it was visually appealing while maintaining accessibility for the elderly.
