Importance of Hydration for the Elderly

This brochure focuses on the importance of proper hydration for elderly health, emphasizing the benefits of staying hydrated and providing practical tips for seniors to improve their water intake. It covers common hydration challenges in seniors and offers solutions such as incorporating hydrating foods and beverages into their daily routine, as well as understanding the signs of dehydration and how to prevent it.
