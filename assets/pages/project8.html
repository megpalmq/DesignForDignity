Fiber

in Senior Health

Fiber plays a crucial role in senior health by promoting digestive wellness, supporting heart health, and helping regulate blood sugar levels. As we age, maintaining a fiber-rich diet becomes even more important to prevent constipation, manage cholesterol, and reduce the risk of chronic diseases like diabetes and heart disease. High-fiber foods such as fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and legumes can help seniors maintain a balanced diet and overall well-being.

The posters aim to raise awareness about the importance of fiber in senior health by providing clear, engaging, and visually appealing information. They highlight the benefits of fiber, suggest easy ways to incorporate it into daily meals, and emphasize its role in promoting a healthy, active lifestyle. By educating seniors and caregivers, these posters encourage better dietary habits that can enhance quality of life.
