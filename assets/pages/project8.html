About the Project

This project focuses on exploring the intersection of food insecurity and mental health among the elderly population. With the aging demographic growing worldwide, many seniors face not only physical challenges but also emotional and psychological burdens. One significant issue is food insecurity, which can lead to nutritional deficiencies, exacerbating existing mental health conditions such as depression, anxiety, and cognitive decline. Through this project, we aim to shed light on the daily struggles elderly individuals face in securing enough nutritious food, and how these challenges influence their mental and emotional well-being. Our goal is to provide practical insights, interventions, and raise awareness about how addressing food insecurity can significantly improve the quality of life for seniors. By combining education with advocacy, this project seeks to engage the community in finding sustainable solutions.

Design Process

  1. Research & Discovery: Our design process began by understanding the needs of the elderly population in relation to food insecurity and its mental health implications. We conducted extensive research, gathering insights from elderly care specialists, nutritionists, and mental health experts
  2. Concept Development:Based on our findings, we conceptualized a design that would educate, inspire, and encourage action. We decided to focus on creating an informative yet visually appealing website that could be easily accessed by seniors and caregivers.
  3. Prototype Testing: We created mockups and prototypes that would represent the challenges seniors face in a sensitive and relatable way. For the color palette, we chose calming, nature-inspired tones to evoke feelings of comfort and trust. The layout was designed to be clean and easy to navigate, ensuring accessibility for users with varying levels of technological literacy.
  4. Final Solution: After multiple iterations, we implemented features such as infographics, resource links, and emotional well-being tips for seniors. The content was designed to provide clear, actionable steps, such as how to access food programs or manage stress related to food insecurity. We included testimonials and stories from real seniors to create a connection with the audience.
