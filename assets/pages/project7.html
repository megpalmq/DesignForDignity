Understanding Food Insecurity and Mental Health in the Elderly

This project focuses on exploring the intersection of food insecurity and mental health among the elderly population. With the aging demographic growing worldwide, many seniors face not only physical challenges but also emotional and psychological burdens. One significant issue is food insecurity, which can lead to nutritional deficiencies, exacerbating existing mental health conditions such as depression, anxiety, and cognitive decline. Through this project, we aim to shed light on the daily struggles elderly individuals face in securing enough nutritious food, and how these challenges influence their mental and emotional well-being. Our goal is to provide practical insights, interventions, and raise awareness about how addressing food insecurity can significantly improve the quality of life for seniors. By combining education with advocacy, this project seeks to engage the community in finding sustainable solutions.
