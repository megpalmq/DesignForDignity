Food Insecurity & Mental Health in Seniors

This project focuses on the intersection of food insecurity and mental health among the elderly population. As the aging demographic grows worldwide, many seniors face not only physical challenges but also emotional and psychological burdens. Food insecurity can lead to nutritional deficiencies that worsen mental health conditions such as depression, anxiety, and cognitive decline.

Through this project, we aim to shed light on the daily struggles elderly individuals face in securing enough nutritious food and how these challenges affect their mental and emotional well-being. Our goal is to provide practical insights and interventions while raising awareness about how addressing food insecurity can significantly improve seniors' quality of life. By combining education with advocacy, we seek to engage the community in finding sustainable solutions.
