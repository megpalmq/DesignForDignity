Food nutrition, diet/amount for the day- What makes up a balanced diet for the elderly?

As seniors age, the body requires specific nutrients to support bone health, muscle function, immune system strength, and energy levels. A well-balanced diet that addresses these needs can significantly improve the quality of life for elderly individuals.
